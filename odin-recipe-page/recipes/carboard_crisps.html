Cardboard Crisps

(for the fancier feasts!)

For the sophisticated palate of the contemporary cat, the allure of a well-aged cardboard box is undeniable. Cardboard Crisps are delicately sourced from the finest parcels and packages, offering a blend of earthy tones and tantalizing textures. With a light crunch that dances on the whiskers, and a nuanced bouquet of woody undertones, this is the flavor experience your cat has been dreaming about. Garnish with a sprinkle of catnip for a culinary journey that takes them from the living room to the lavender fields of Provence. It's the purrfect treat for the cultured kitty!

Prep Time
1 shake of a whisker
Cook Time
10 Mews
Total Time
At least 1 nap
Servings
20

Ingredients

  • One high-quality cardboard box
  • With optional catnip for a better experience

Directions:

  1. Take all the umimportant cat toys and food out of the box
  2. Put the box in a sunny spot
  3. After a few hours of sunlight, break the box into medium sized pieces
  4. Set up the area
  5. Final step is to eat or chew on them to your hearts delight
