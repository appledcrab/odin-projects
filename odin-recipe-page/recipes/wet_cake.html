|| Wet Cake ||

(for the fancier feasts!)

Great for birthday events!

Prep Time
-10
Cook Time
PUT ME DOWN
Total Time
please 3:
Servings
maybe 2

Ingredients

  • One can of wet food (brand of choice)
  • store-bought candle
  • one good bowl
  • Joy.

Tiara optional.

    Directions:

  1. Take the can of wet food.
  2. Turn over the can of wet food
  3. Hit it until it comes out
  4. Forget you didn't do this over a table
  5. or a bowl.
  6. move it to your bowl
  7. Add the candle!
